Parkinson’s Disease diagnosis using
Ensemble Techniques

Parkinson’s Disease (PD) is a degenerative neurological disorder marked by decreased dopamine levels in the brain. It manifests itself through a deterioration of movement, including the presence of tremors and stiffness. Goal is to classify the patients into the respective labels using the attributes from their voice recordings. Since there is no definitive laboratory test to diagnose PD, diagnosis is often difficult, particularly in the early stages when motor effects are not yet severe.
